To The Center Of The Earth is a twin stick arcade shooter where it's your goal to get as much treasure from the earth as possible!

Take on the role of the Drill Pilot, and collect as much treasure on your way down as possible. But be careful, as the platform you ride controls your life support, so make sure to dig up fuel to keep the run alive!

To the Center of the Earth was made as an entry for the Indie City All-stars Game Jam. Jam was hosted by Indie City Games in July 2024. Game was chosen by members of Iron Galaxy to be one of the ten games featured at an in-person showcase at Night City.

Design, Programming, Art: Harrison Kratz
Sound Design: Ari Hyman
Composition: Olive McMillan
