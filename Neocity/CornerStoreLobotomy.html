CORNER STORE LOBOTOMY


Corner Store Lobotomy is a Game Jam project made for the Indie City Chicaghoul 2024 Game Jam!

You just got a job at a corner store in a strange part of town. Everything seems fine on the outside, but in the back room of the store is an offering of different drugs, organs, and other monstrous items for sale!

In this point and click puzzle game, Its your job to administer and perform surgical operations, to make sure our guests get the most out of our products! Inject guests with mysterious syringes and cut open their body to perform dubious transplant proceedures. Your percision and timing will be key to becoming the next employee of the month!

CHICAGHOUL 2024 WINNER


Corner Store Lobotomy was voted Best Game at the Chicaghoul 2024 Showcase event held at Night City
Some of the sponsors of the event included GX Games, Game Maker, DreadXP, and The Midwest Game Industry Confrence who invited me to attend MDEV 2024 for winning the competition.
