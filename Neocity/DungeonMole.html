Dungeon Mole is an experimental game concept that mixes Tower Defense style combat with the exploration aspects of a classic Dungeon Crawler. You play as Clay, a small garden mole seeking to protect his home from the mechanized bugs that burrow beneath the ground. Delve deep into the ground, exploring a maze like cave network, and fighting off the incoming insect threats. However, Clay is unable to attack the enemies himself, but the seeds you find on your journey can be planted to defend yourself from oncoming attackers!
